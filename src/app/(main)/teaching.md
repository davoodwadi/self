# https://Spreed.chat
I created spreed.chat So that my students can easily evaluate different large language models and business domains and identify different biases, decision making processes. This interactive tool enables easy A B testing and executing control experiments with large language models as the subject of analysis.

# https://behavioral-llm.com
I created behavioral-llm As a tool to enable marketing researchers to design control experiments, including rating and choice experiments to test out different large language models as consumer decision-making agents.
